let me set the scene:

  • 27 years old and just moved to melbourne
  • a bit of time on my hands
  • an opportunity to upskill

the first time i touched html was trying to find a tumblr theme in 2013, and the last time (before this website) was in my 2016 info101 class at university.

i’m hoping this website can give me an outlet for my thoughts, but also showcase my skills.
